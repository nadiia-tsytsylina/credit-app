About Empower Perks

Eligible customers can select and activate a perk in-app prior to making purchases with the Empower debit card in order to get cashback. Please note that only one perk can be activated at a time, but this can be swapped for another perk at any time.

To choose your perk:

  1. On your Accounts tab, tap on the "Add" button in the "Choose Your Perk" bubble under your Empower Card balance
  2. Select the perk of your choice
  3. Tap "Add Perk" to activate it
  4. Make your purchase with your Empower debit card and earn your cashback!

To change your perk:

  1. Tap on "Change" in the bubble below your Empower Card balance
  2. Select the perk you want to choose
  3. Tap "Add Perk"
