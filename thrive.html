Thrive

Why don’t I see the option to apply for Thrive?

Thrive is currently in beta, so only existing Empower customers living in certain states have been invited to apply at the moment. Our team is working hard to expand this to more customers, and we expect to launch Thrive to more customers across the U.S. in the second half of 2023. Please stay tuned for more information!

What is Thrive and how is it different from Cash Advance?

Thrive is a line of credit provided by one of our bank partners, FinWise Bank, Member FDIC. Unlike Cash Advance, Thrive is a credit product. This means that your payment history and account information are reported to the credit bureaus every month, and you will see it as a tradeline on your credit report. Also unlike Cash Advance, you may be charged interest (up to 35.99% APR) on the money you draw on your line of credit, and late fees may apply.
